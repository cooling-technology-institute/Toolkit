Welcome to CTI Toolkit 3.2

 

 

 

The Cooling Technology Institute (CTI) has been publishing its "Blue Book" for cooling tower thermal design for over 25 years, and its Version 1.0 software product since 1999. Now CTI is proud to present its Toolkit software, Version 3.1.

 

Members of the Cooling Tower Institute’s Performance and Technology committee developed the underlying computer code used throughout this application.

 

The basic tab functions of Psychrometrics / Merkel / Demand Curves / Mechanical Draft Performance Curve are designed to facilitate cooling tower calculations and to essentially replace the hard copy "Blue Book". Through the use of a connected printer, "Blue Book" quality prints can be produced.
