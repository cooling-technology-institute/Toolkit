Merkel Tab Usage

 

 

The inputs required for the calculation are:

  • Entering (or Hot) water temperature, (T1)

  • Leaving (or Cold) water temperature, (T2)

  • Wet bulb temperature of the incoming air, (WBT)

  • Mass ratio of water to air, (L/G)

  • Site Barometric Pressure or Altitude

 

The output of the calculation is the dimensionless grouping ‘KaV/L’, which is a measure of the required duty imposed on a cooling tower.
